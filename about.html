About this work

The app and statistical analyses were done by Colas Droin, PhD student in the Naef Lab, under the supervision of Dr. Felix Naef.

This work is done in collaboration with the Itzkovitz lab. Experiments, data collection and wrangling were made by

Jakob El-Kholtei and Keren Bahar Halpen under the supervision of Dr. Shalev Itzkovitz.
